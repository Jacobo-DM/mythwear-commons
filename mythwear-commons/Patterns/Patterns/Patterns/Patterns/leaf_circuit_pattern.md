# Leaf Circuit · Wearable Nervous System  
Commons Blueprint v1.0

## Purpose
A soft, symbolic nervous system map for tracing breath, tension, and emotion.  
No electronics required.

## Materials
- Leaf-shaped fabric piece
- Cord or yarn for central and branching “veins”
- Needle, thread
- Optional beads or bells

## Pattern Steps
1. Cut fabric into a leaf or teardrop shape.
2. Stitch a central “spine vein” down the center.
3. Add branching veins at angles like a leaf.
4. Mark intersections with beads or bell nodes.

## Use
- Breath tracing  
- Sensory calming  
- Movement warm-ups  
- Emotional mapping
