## Purpose
Soft, minimal foot and ankle wraps to cultivate grounding, quiet steps, and stability.

## Materials
- Soft stretch fabric
- Optional textured sole layer
- Needle, thread

## Tabi Steps
1. Cut sole and upper pieces, leaving a toe split.
2. Sew upper to sole; reinforce toe split.
3. Add simple tie or wraparound closure.

## Ankle Band Steps
1. Cut a band long enough to wrap around the ankle once or twice.
2. Stitch a gentle spiral to symbolize grounding flow.
3. Add soft ties or Velcro.

## Use
Balance practice, grounding, mindful walking, Rage Dance basics.
