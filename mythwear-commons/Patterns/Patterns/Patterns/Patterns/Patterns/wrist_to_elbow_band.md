# Wrist–Elbow Band · Force Path Trainer  
Commons Blueprint v1.0

## Purpose
A soft band linking wrist and elbow to teach alignment and the path of force.  
Used in Rage Dance foundations.

## Materials
- Soft cloth strips
- Optional padding
- Thread and needle

## Components
- **Wrist cuff**  
- **Elbow cuff**  
- **Connecting band**

## Pattern Steps
1. Sew wrist cuff and elbow cuff to comfortable loops.
2. Attach a fabric band from the outside of the wrist cuff to the outside of the elbow cuff.
3. Add a single guiding stitch line down the band.
4. Optional: add a bell at the elbow cuff for sound feedback.

## Use
Slow or sharp movements reveal tension, softness, or alignment.
