The Spoovel · V1

The Spoovel is a hybrid tool for scooping, sifting, and gentle excavation. Originally conceived in the Fox’s Anvil series as a children’s story-object, it grew into a simple prototype for field inquiry and playful learning.

Materials

  • Wooden spoon or thrifted scoop
  • Small metal trowel
  • Twine or natural cordage
  • Non-toxic finish (optional)

Assembly (Simple)

Bind the scoop and trowel together at the handles using twine or cord. Ensure a comfortable grip and angle the tools to suit intended use.

Uses

  • Field sampling
  • Soil or sand exploration
  • Sensory play and imaginative inquiry
  • Carrying “story seeds” in games and lessons

This pattern is intentionally incomplete — an invitation for children, educators, and makers to adapt it freely.
