MythWear Patterns

Text-only, body-worn blueprints released under the MythWear Commons License v1.1. MythWear patterns are garments and wearable tools only: hoods, masks, bands, tabi, and leaf-circuits stitched into clothing — nothing that is not worn on the body.

  • MythWear Hood · Arc–Spine Pattern
    A soft hood that frames the face with three anchor points at brow and temples, and a spine-channel running over crown and down the back.
    Includes optional “Beaded Sanctuary Module”: a quiet interior of wooden beads along the rim, crown, or spine-line — a portable cave for grounding, reflection, and classroom calm.
  • RootMask / RootFace · Nopal Lattice
    A ventilated face frame inspired by cactus rib structures — breath-first design, soft light, open sight.
  • Leaf Circuit · Wearable Nervous System
    Subtle spine-and-branch lines stitched or corded onto garments to map attention, movement, and flow.
    • Wrist–Elbow Band · Force Path Trainer
    Dual soft cuffs linked by a band that teaches the path of force through the forearm — a training companion for RageDance and mindful play.
  • Tabi + Ankle Band · Grounding Feet
    Split-toe tabi with a gentle Achilles band — “listening shoes” for quiet steps, balance, and grounding.
