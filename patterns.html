# MythWear Patterns Text-only, body-worn blueprints released under the [MythWear Commons License v1.1](mythwear-license.html). MythWear garments are **body-centered**: hoods, masks, bands, tabi, and leaf-circuits stitched into clothing — nothing that is not worn on the body. ## Core Wearable Patterns - [MythWear Hood · Arc–Spine Pattern](mythwear_hood_pattern.txt) A soft hood with spine-line channel and three anchor points. _Includes optional “Beaded Sanctuary Module”: a quiet interior of wooden beads along the rim, crown, or spine-line — a portable cave for grounding, reflection, and classroom calm._ - [RootMask / RootFace · Nopal Lattice](rootmask_structure.txt) A ventilated face frame inspired by cactus rib structures — breath-first design, soft light, open sight. - [Leaf Circuit · Wearable Nervous System](leaf_circuit_pattern.txt) A stitched or painted map of flow — a body-based nervous system for grounding and movement. - [Wrist–Elbow Band · Force Path Trainer](wrist_to_elbow_band.txt) Dual soft cuffs linked by a band that teaches the path of force through the forearm — a training companion for RageDance and mindful play. - [Tabi + Ankle Band · Grounding Feet](tabi_and_ankle_band.txt) Split-toe tabi with a gentle Achilles band — “listening shoes” for quiet steps, balance, and grounding. ## Modules - [Beaded Sanctuary Module](modules/beaded_sanctuary.txt) Portable cave interior for hoods and garments — grounding beads along rim, crown, or spine. Can be added to MythWear Hood, RootMask side panels, Leaf Circuit spine lines, and more. ## Guides - [Teacher’s Notes: Beaded Sanctuary Hood](guides/teachers_notes_sanctuary_hood.txt) Classroom guide for trauma-aware, nonverbal regulation and calm.
